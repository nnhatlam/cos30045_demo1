Understanding Energy Ratings

All our televisions are rated according to international energy efficiency standards. Look for the energy rating badge on each product:

  • A++ Rating: Best-in-class efficiency, lowest running costs
  • A+ Rating: Excellent efficiency with great performance
  • A Rating: Good efficiency, reliable and economical

Each TV listing includes annual energy cost savings compared to standard models, helping you make an informed decision.
